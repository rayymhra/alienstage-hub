About This Archive

What is AlienStage Hub?

AlienStage Hub is a fan-curated archive dedicated to the Alien Stage animated series. It serves as a comprehensive resource for character lore, fan interpretations, visual creations, and community theories.

This project was built out of love for the characters and stories that Alien Stage has brought to life — and the voices that were silenced on its stage.

Disclaimer

This is a fan-made website and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the official Alien Stage team or its creators.

All character lore belongs to the original series. Fanart is credited to respective artists. Theories represent community interpretations, not canon.
