About This Archive

What is AlienStage Hub?

A fan-curated archive dedicated to the Alien Stage animated series. A comprehensive resource for character lore, fan interpretations, visual creations, and community theories covering all 25 documented individuals.

Built out of love for the characters and stories that Alien Stage brought to life — and the voices that were silenced on its stage.

Disclaimer

This is a fan-made website and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the official Alien Stage team or its creators.

All character lore belongs to the original series. Fanart is credited to respective artists. Theories represent community interpretations, not canon. Supporting character data includes fan-interpreted information.
